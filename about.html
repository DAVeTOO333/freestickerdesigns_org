About This Site

FreeStickerDesigns.org is a free community library of sticker designs. Download anything here, print it yourself or use a sticker printer, and put it wherever feels right.

This site doesn't have a political agenda. It's open to all kinds of designs — funny, artistic, weird, activist, nostalgic, pop culture, whatever. The only rule: no hate. No content that targets or demeans people based on who they are.

All designs are submitted by artists who want their work out in the world. They retain their rights — you just get to download and print.

If you've got something worth sticking on things, submit it.

Questions or want to get in touch? Email us at hello@freestickerdesigns.org.
